Our Story

Where Bangalore's warmth meets a Navi Mumbai morning.

Dakshin Tiffin Club brings the warmth of traditional Bangalore tiffin culture to Navi Mumbai. From buttery benne dosas to aromatic filter coffee, every dish is crafted for comfort, nostalgia, and authenticity — exactly the way grandmothers in Malleshwaram and Basavanagudi have made them for decades.
